Vintage Lemonade

Description

Vintage lemonade is a refreshing and classic beverage that combines the tangy taste of freshly squeezed lemons with the sweetness of simple syrup, balanced by a touch of cold water.

Ingredients

  • 5 lemons
  • 1 ¼ cups white sugar
  • 1 ¼ quarts water

Steps

  1. Juice the 5 lemons to extract as much juice as possible, and set the juice aside.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the 1 ¼ cups of white sugar with 1 ¼ quarts of water. Heat over medium until the sugar is completely dissolved, creating a simple syrup. Allow the mixture to cool.
  3. In a large pitcher, mix the lemon juice with the cooled simple syrup. Stir well, then chill in the refrigerator. Serve over ice, garnished with lemon slices if desired.
