Sparkling Raspberry Lemonade

Description

Sparkling Raspberry Lemonade is a vibrant and refreshing drink that combines the tartness of lemon juice with the sweetness of fresh raspberries, all topped off with bubbly sparkling water. Perfect for summer gatherings, this effervescent beverage is both delightful and easy to prepare, offering a burst of fruity flavor in every sip.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 cup lemon juice
  • 4 cups sparkling water

Steps

  1. In a blender, puree the fresh raspberries until smooth. Strain the puree through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the seeds.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine the raspberry puree and lemon juice. Stir well to mix.
  3. Add the sparkling water to the pitcher and gently stir. Serve over ice, garnished with fresh raspberries or lemon slices if desired.
