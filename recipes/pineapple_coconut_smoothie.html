Pinapple Coconut Smoothie

Description

The Pineapple Coconut Smoothie is a creamy and tropical delight that blends juicy pineapple chunks with rich coconut milk and a touch of honey for natural sweetness. This smoothie is not only delicious but also packed with vitamins, making it an excellent choice for a refreshing and healthy treat any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Steps

  1. Place the fresh pineapple chunks in a blender.
  2. Add the coconut milk and honey to the blender.
  3. Blend on high until smooth and creamy. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
